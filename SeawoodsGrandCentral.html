Seawoods Grand Central

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

The project is located in Navi Mumbai around Seawoods Station. The total BUA is 35,00,000 sq. ft. There are total 21 buildings with 13 different types.
Challenges

As the Residential project is located adjacent to Seawoods Darave railway station, some portion of the Residential part came under entry and exit points of the railways station. For that particular portion, MEP services had to be designed and executed prior to Residential project. With respect to the Airspace utility building, locating DG sets and its exhaust is challenging. As the entrance level is at - 2.7 metre, Bringing MEP services from outside to the building was also complicated. Some of the towers are also going to be constructed above podium level on existing railway parking. It was not feasible to go down due to railway parking extent which is excluded from residential premises. Diversion of sewage pipes up to STP was difficult since there was only one STP for those towers situated at corner location.

Solution

Portion to be handed over to Railway is demarked and clear segregation in MEP services for these services and Residential services are maintained. As there are multiple towers and single STP for those towers situated at the corner, diversion of sewage pipes to STP was challenging. To overcome the situation, all plumbing drainage pipes are diverted at Lower Ground ceiling level and connected to external sewer network. External Sewage network is laid all across periphery of the plot and then it is connected to STP. Since the Airport Authority has not permitted for 90 metre desired height, fire fighting services are designed for considering both the possibilities of 90 metre and 60 metre height.
