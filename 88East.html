88 East

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

88 East is a prestigious residential high-rise project launched by Tata Housing in Alipore, Kolkata. The project consists of a 150 meter tall tower, six levels of podium parking, 38 floors residential apartments and Amenity areas at terrace and podium level. Total BUA of project is 7,00,000 sq. ft.
Challenges

The plan aspect ratio was greater than 2.5 and the slenderness ratio of height to core was around 9. The soil was very soft and weak. With respect to MEP services, the client asked our team to validate the various system of HVAC, PHE services in order to bring the most cost and space efficient solution for the project.

Solution

We used scattered shear wall system with appropriate gust wind and wind tunnel analysis. Appropriate sheer wall ductility study was conducted. We suggested 5 meters deep piles and provided fully optimized layout. The entire Structure- Arch- MEP coordination was on BIM platform. We also provided all design justification, costing and solution in order to meet the most efficient solution to the client. As per local body norms, DG exhaust outlet was coordinated with other services and exhaust was places at building top through the dedicated shaft.
