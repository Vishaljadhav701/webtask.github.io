SP Infocity

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

It is a Grade A office building (LEED GOLD) with 11 floors of parking and 6 levels of parking. The total BUA was approximately 1.3 million sq. ft.
Challenges

Long span structure with demand of high clear headroom in habitable area. It had long overall dimension and expansion issues. The cantilever span was huge (Approx 5m).

Solution

We proposed flat PT slab connected to band beam only at one edge to give high clear headroom. We avoided expansion joint and did temperature analysis. For the cantilever we did long term deflection check and tapered RCC section.
